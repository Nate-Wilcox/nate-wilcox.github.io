More About Me

I fell in love with programming and computer science as a whole in 2017 when I participated in the Joint Cyber Analysis Course(JCAC) through the US Navy. Once I entered the Naval reserves in 2018 I went back to school, switching from my original major of mechanical engineering. I graduated from Seattle Central College with an Associates of Sciences - Computer Science in 2021. Since then I have transferred to Seattle Pacific University to finish my undergraduate degree. I was a software development intern for Best Buy HQ through the summer of 2022.

I'm highly energetic and motivated person and I love to work as a member or a leader of a team. I especially enjoy working with changing projects and taking them forward with rapid pace but high precision. I am normally pretty quiet until I feel comfortable but once I feel comfortable with a team I am a great communicator and thoroughly enjoy working with others.

I am also in the process of creating a photography and videography business with a close friend due to our mutual love of those creative forms.
